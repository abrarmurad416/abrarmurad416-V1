stickynotes.ed | Co-founder

stickynotes.ed is a student-led non-profit organization that offers advice from students to students. The premise of stickynotes.ed is to influence and direct highschool students to university or college programs that suit their passions and interests while still being sustainable. We offer volunteering hours to highschool students with a selection of roles that suit their interest. Currently, we have 200+ followers on Instagram, which includes students from numerous PDSB schools and npos on Instagram. As the Co-founder, I have interviewed a master’s com sci student on our YouTube channel, created a company page using Notion, networked with 20+ orgs to promote our page, created a discord server that offers advice and résumé help, and created a LinkedIn page.
